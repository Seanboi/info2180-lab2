Bleach

September 31,2024 by Sean Spence

Bleach, a piece of media that has been ongoing since 2004 and has been a part ofthe hearts of many fans of anime or japanese media. This is a cartoon that is best known for its Main character Ichigo Kurosaki, as he one day is granted a power by a mysterious woman and becomes a shinigami. A shinigami is roughly translated to a God of Death, however he is not evil and he guides lost souls to the after life. Many evil monsters and people wish to feed on these souls before they can reach there and it is his duty to protect them.

Bleach is widely considered one of the best anime out there compared to the likes of naruto, one piece and Dragon Ball Z, it is a heavily favourited anime by many, including me of course. The amazing fights and emotional depth of the story gets you intertwined in the life of this random character that is on our screens.
